Our Business

Nordic Global Shippers's Core Values

Safety: We aspire to create safe environments both on and off the roads. Safety is paramount in Nordic Global Shippers and affects everyone we interact with.

Customer Focus: We understand that each item is meaningful to every sender and its receiver. We are the messengers who deliver each of our customers’ stories, and we take responsibility by helping them get their message across.

Teamwork: We embrace our similarities and differences, and continuously learn, grow, and work together. We understand that each of us plays a role in getting the job done, and we’re happy to do it as a team.

Communication: We do our best to communicate well – even in high-pressure situations. We treat every person with respect and we are always our most authentic selves.

Leadership: We lead by simply leaving a positive difference in the lives of individuals around us by acting with purpose, making a call, and owning it.
